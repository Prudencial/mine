WHO ARE WE

We are a locally initiated water supply and agricultural biased company
.
It is a company formed from the interest of its directors especially Mr. BONIFACE MAZIKU who has long time worked in community development especially in supply of water for small gardens and domestic use since 1979. The problem of water supply for agriculture and domestic use have been following down and down due to weather change. Formerly since 1950s there had been 3 to 4 years of drought in every decade and this has been affecting the underground water table but as climate continue being deteriorated due to environment degradation, the rains till now are no longer reliable nor predictable by local farmers. So the dependence on deep water supply has been growing and the expenses of doing survey by use of digital machines has not been friendly to local people. So Mr. Boniface Maziku since 1979 has been interested in making deep practical study in Divine water survey method which is easy and cheap. And since 2016 Mr. Maziku decided to do serious surveys on voluntary and cheaper way till this time of the year 2020 decided to register this company with his son Mr. Peter Maziku who is construction engineer. Till the year 2020 Mr. Maziku has surveyed over 165 wells and supervised drilling
of about 40 wells out of which there is a success by 85 % of the drilled wells under his supervision. There is a special report on details of surveyed and drilled wells.

Therefore this company was formed to help local well owners to have cheap and accurate water survey and drilling services to enable them have adequate clean and safe water for human consumption and health benefits also for irrigation and animal use for their economic growth. .

OUR SERVICES

  • Underground water and mineral detection by use of copper rods as means of divine underground survey and survey report preparation.
  • Studding land to allocate and design small pond or dam construction for agriculture
  • Supervising drilling works that may be done according to the surveyed data
  • Water pump installation, repair and flashing works to restore old wells
  • Advising and providing generator and irrigation tools /equipment and agricultural advises to well owners
  • Conducting theory and practical training of new surveyors and drivers of drilling rigs especially for enabling them to drill in difficult soil formation
  • Doing scientific research on the best use of copper rod divine water survey method
  • Designing local tools and equipment for water detectors, drilling tools, irrigation tools and free energy generators and other innovations.
  • Buying and selling all water use needed equipment by our customers.
  • Consultancy and study on water and agriculture works and businesses

OUR VISION

To be a leading global provider of water survey and supply services for domestic use, animal and agriculture uses.  .

MISSION

  • Conduct deeper scientific study on divine water and mineral survey using copper rods and prepare a useful booklet for training surveyors in its use.
  • Use electronic engineers to design and produce a digital water and mineral detectors that work aligned with copper rod divine method.
  • Train surveyors in divine survey skills and improve skills of drillers who use muddy and air driven drilling rigs.
  • Fabricate and produce local drilling equipment, irrigation tools, free energy generators and other innovative tools for water supply and agricultural improvements
  • Provide water pumps, generators and water irrigation needed tools and advise our well and pond owners on how to do simple and viable mean irrigation works to improve food supply
  • Buy and sell all tools and equipment and other supplies as related to water and agriculture equipment and other supplies.
  • Train people in basic ethics of development so that they can be able to manage well their projects and incomes for promoting and protecting human life and dignity..
